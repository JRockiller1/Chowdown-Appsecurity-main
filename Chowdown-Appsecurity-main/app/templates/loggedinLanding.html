Why Us

Why Choose Our Platform

01

Location

Our vendors are able to deliver food in a fast and timely manner within at least a 15km radius in Singapore.

02

Quality

We guarantee the best quality of food for our valued customers.


We will send one of our food critics to evaluate the food of vendors that applies with us. Only once they have passed our quality check, will they be able to sign up with us.

03

Aiding Smaller vendors

Smaller vendors are more often overlooked by consumers when it comes to picking out food as they are unknown and placed in a remote location such as the void deck.


Due to this, we advocate and deliver for these smaller companies who may not have the branding benefit of common big-named food companies in the industry such as Mc Donalds.
